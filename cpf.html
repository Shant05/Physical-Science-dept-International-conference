Invitation to Submit

The International Conference on Applied Statistics and Data Analytics (**ICASDA 2026**) invites researchers, practitioners, and students to submit high-quality papers presenting original research in all areas of Applied Statistics and Data Analytics. We seek contributions that address fundamental concepts, emerging technologies, and real-world solutions across various disciplines.

All accepted and registered papers will be published in the conference proceedings and submitted for inclusion in major indexing databases (e.g., Scopus, Web of Science).

Submission Requirements

  • **Paper Length:** Submissions must be between 6 to 8 pages, including figures, tables, and references.
  • **Format:** Use the standard IEEE two-column conference template (available on the Submission page).
  • **Originality:** All submitted papers must report original, unpublished research. Simultaneous submissions to other conferences or journals are strictly prohibited.
  • **Review Process:** Papers will undergo a rigorous double-blind peer-review process by the Program Committee.
