Organizing Institutions & Departments

Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Department of Statistics (Established 1999)

Attained Deemed to be University status in July 2025. Reaccredited with **'A++' (NAAC)** and ranked **34th in NIRF 2025**. The Department of Statistics has successfully hosted three National Conferences and now presents its first International Conference.

Focus: Theory and application of statistical techniques to UG/PG students across diverse disciplines.

Bangalore University

Department of Statistics (Established 1968)

A pioneer in Statistics education since 1968, offering M.Sc. and Ph.D. programmes. The Department is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and professional development, integrating both theoretical and applied dimensions of Statistics.

Focus: Quality education, impactful research, and collaborations with statistical organizations and industries.

Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam

School of Mathematics and Statistics (Established 2020)

A vibrant academic unit dedicated to the advancement of mathematical sciences and statistical methodologies. The School plays a pivotal role in nurturing analytical thinking and actively engages with emerging trends through seminars, workshops, and conferences.

Focus: Advancing mathematical sciences, statistical methodologies, and nurturing analytical problem-solving skills.
