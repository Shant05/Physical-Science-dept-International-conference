The Conference Vision

Objectives

  • Provide a **global platform** for researchers to share innovative ideas in applied statistics and data analytics.
  • Explore the **convergence** of statistical and analytical techniques to address complex real-world challenges.
  • Foster **collaboration** among academia, industry, and government organizations.
  • Disseminate insights into the societal and commercial implications of emerging **data-driven technologies**.

The **International Conference on Applied Statistics and Data Analytics (ICASDA 2026)** is a premier event uniting experts across statistics, data science, and related disciplines. The conference emphasizes advancing research that bridges **theoretical foundations** with **real-world applications**.

Submissions are encouraged that contribute to the theoretical development of applied statistics and data analytics, or demonstrate practical implementations in diverse fields such as **healthcare, finance, sustainable development, and intelligent systems**.

Organizing Institutions & Departments

Kristu Jayanti(Deemed to be University)

Department of Statistics

Established in 1999, Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University), managed by the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI) Fathers, St. Joseph Province, Kottayam, Kerala, has emerged as a centre of excellence in higher education. Accredited with an 'A++' grade by NAAC (Third Cycle, 2021) and ranked 34th in NIRF 2025, the university offers diverse programmes across Science, Arts, Commerce, Management, and Law. The Department of Statistics provides a strong academic foundation integrating statistical theory, mathematics, and computational applications through programmes such as B.Sc. Statistics with Mathematics (STMM), Statistics with Economics (STEC), and Statistics with Computer Science (STCS).

Focus: Comprehensive training in Descriptive Statistics, Probability, Data Analysis using R, Statistical Inference, Regression, and Experimental Design. Hands-on exposure through workshops, guest lectures, industrial visits, research projects, and social outreach initiatives like SOP and CLP. Emphasis on experiential and self-paced learning through MOOCs and NPTEL courses. Preparation for diverse careers in Data Science, Analytics, Finance, Research, Healthcare, Technology, and Academia.

Bangalore University

Department of Statistics

The Department of Statistics, established in 1968 at Bangalore University, Bengaluru, commenced with the B.Sc. (Hons.) programme and expanded in 1969 with the introduction of the M.Sc. programme. Since its inception, the Department has been committed to excellence in teaching, research, and professional development, offering M.Sc. and Ph.D. programmes that integrate both theoretical and applied dimensions of Statistics. With a focus on quality education, impactful research, and scholarly publications, the Department actively collaborates with statistical organizations, research and development establishments, industries, and other academic institutions, thereby advancing statistical knowledge and its applications across diverse fields.

Focus: Comprehensive training in Probability Theory, Statistical Inference, Linear Models, Multivariate Analysis, and Data Analytics. Learning integrated with applications in social, natural, and economic sciences. Hands-on exposure through research projects, workshops, seminars, and consultancy activities. Emphasis on computational statistics and modern statistical software. Preparation for diverse careers in Data Science, Research, Education, Government, and Industry.

Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam

School of Mathematics and Statistics

The School of Mathematics and Statistics was established in 2020 at Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala, is a vibrant academic unit committed to the advancement of mathematical sciences, statistical methodologies, and their diverse applications. The School plays a pivotal role in nurturing analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, equipping students for a wide spectrum of professional careers and higher academic pursuits. Alongside its academic programmes, the School actively organizes seminars, workshops, and conferences to engage with emerging trends, challenges, and innovations in mathematics and statistics. These initiatives create a platform for students and faculty to interact with eminent scholars and practitioners, fostering a dynamic and intellectually enriching environment within the School.

Focus: To provide teaching, research and training in theoretical and applied areas of mathematical sciences with interdisciplinary applications. Mathematics and Statistics are fundamental disciplines of knowledge and constitute most important tools for all programmes of study and research as well as science & technology, social sciences, health and welfare, industry, business and management.

Host Institution

Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Kristu Jayanti **Deemed to be University** has been a beacon of learning and innovation since its inception in **1999** as an affiliated institution under Bangalore University. The Institution is managed by the Christian Missionaries belonging to the order of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), St. Joseph Province, Kottayam, Kerala. Marking a significant milestone in its journey, Kristu Jayanti attained the **'autonomous' status** in the year **2013**. As per the notification dated July 08, 2025, vide letter No. 9-13/2025 - U.3(A), Kristu Jayanti has been conferred the status of Deemed to be University under section 3 of UGC Act, 1956, by the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India, under General Category.
