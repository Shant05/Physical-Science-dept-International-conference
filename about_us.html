The Conference Vision

Objectives

  • To provide a global forum for researchers to share innovative ideas in Applied Statistics and Data Analytics.
  • To explore the convergence of statistical and data analytic techniques for solving complex real-world challenges.
  • To foster collaboration between academia, industry, and government sectors.
  • To disseminate knowledge about the societal and commercial implications of emerging data-driven technologies.

The **International Conference on Applied Statistics and Data Analytics (ICASDA 2026)** is set to be a premier event bringing together experts from various domains of statistics, data science, and related fields. Our focus is on fostering groundbreaking research that bridges theoretical foundations with practical applications.

We welcome submissions that contribute to the theoretical development of Applied Statistics and Data Analytics, as well as those that describe its successful deployment in areas like healthcare, finance, sustainable development, and smart systems.

Organizing Departments

Department of Statistics, Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Established in 1999, has been dedicated to imparting knowledge in both the theory and application of statistical techniques to undergraduate and postgraduate students across diverse disciplines. The Department consistently strives to enrich the academic community by organizing conferences, seminars, and workshops.

Visit KJC Stats Dept
Department of Statistics, Bangalore University

Established in 1968 at Bangalore University, Bengaluru, commenced with the B.Sc. (Hons.) programme and expanded in 1969 with the introduction of the M.Sc. programme. Since its inception, the Department has been committed to excellence in teaching, research, and professional development.

Visit BU Website
School of Mathematics & Statistics, Mahatma Gandhi University

The School of Mathematics and Statistics was established in 2020 at Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, Kerala, is a vibrant academic unit committed to the advancement of mathematical sciences, statistical methodologies, and their diverse applications.

Host Institutions

Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Kristu Jayanti Deemed to be University has been a beacon of learning and innovation since its inception in 1999 as an affiliated institution under Bangalore University. The Institution is managed by the Christian Missionaries belonging to the order of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), St. Joseph Province, Kottayam, Kerala. Marking a significant milestone in its journey, Kristu Jayanti attained the 'autonomous' status in the year 2013. As per the notification dated July 08, 2025, vide letter No. 9-13/2025 - U.3(A), Kristu Jayanti has been conferred the status of Deemed to be University under section 3 of UGC Act, 1956, by the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India, under General Category.
