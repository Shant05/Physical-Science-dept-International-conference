Guidelines and Submission Portal

Follow the steps below to successfully submit your research paper for ICASDA 2026.

Templates and Formatting

All submissions must be prepared strictly using the official conference template. Failure to adhere to the formatting guidelines may result in rejection.

  • IEEE Manuscript Templates (Primary) Download Word/LaTeX
  • Paper Length Restriction 6 to 8 Pages Max
  • Review Type Double-Blind Review

Access the Submission System

We use the EasyChair system for paper submission and review management.

Go to EasyChair Portal

(Ensure you select 'ICASDA 2026' upon logging in)

Step-by-Step Submission Process

Step 1: Create Account

Create an author account on the EasyChair submission platform.

Step 2: Format Paper

Strictly follow the **IEEE Template** and ensure **anonymity** for the double-blind review.

Step 3: Select Topics

Correctly select the relevant conference tracks/topics during the submission process.

Step 4: Wait for Review

After submission, the paper enters the peer-review cycle. Await the acceptance notification.

Important Notice for Accepted Authors

Upon acceptance, authors will be required to submit the **Camera-Ready version** (non-anonymous) and complete the **registration process** (payment) by the deadline to ensure inclusion in the conference proceedings.

For any technical difficulties with the submission system, please email: ncci@kristujayanti.com
