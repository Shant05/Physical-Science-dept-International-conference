Guidelines and Submission Portal

Follow the step-by-step process below to successfully submit your research paper for ICASDA 2026.

Templates & Formatting

All submissions must strictly follow the official conference template. Non-compliance with formatting guidelines may result in rejection.

  • IEEE Manuscript Templates (Primary) Download Word/LaTeX
  • Paper Length Restriction 6 to 8 Pages Max
  • Review Type Double-Blind Review

Access the Submission System

We use the EasyChair platform for secure paper submission and peer review management.

Go to EasyChair Portal

(Select ICASDA 2026 after logging in)

Step-by-Step Submission Process

Step 1: Create Account

Register an author account on the EasyChair submission platform.

Step 2: Format Paper

Use the official IEEE template and maintain anonymity for review.

Step 3: Select Topics

Choose the appropriate conference tracks or topics during submission.

Step 4: Wait for Review

Your paper enters the peer-review cycle. Wait for the acceptance notification.

Important Notice for Accepted Authors

Upon acceptance, authors must submit the Camera-Ready Version (non-anonymous) and complete the Registration & Payment process before the deadline to ensure inclusion in the proceedings.
